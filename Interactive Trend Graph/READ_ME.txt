Graph 
	- All posts by date and score/reach per Page 
	- To see if there is a certain trend/behaviour to viral posts

Score/Reach Points (calculated with weights)
	- like * 0.25 + (total_reacts - like) * 0.5 + comments * 0.75 + shares 

Tooltip 
	- Shows [PageName] Title : Score -- Datetime
	

Functionalities:
	- Click on legend to show/hide graph
	- Scroll and Pan
	- Zoom
